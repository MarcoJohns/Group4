Adventures Utah
Now that you have arrived, kick-back, take a breath of fresh air, and have fun. As you explore our charming town you may notice the unique blend of new and old buildings and elements from the town's silver mining era and skiing history are seamlessly woven into the town's more modern architecture. Keep an eye out for rings, symbols, flags, and statues displayed from Park City's participation in the 2002 Winter Olympic Games. In fact you can even take the ride of a lifetime on the bobsled and experience what it is like to go down the Olympic track at the Utah Olympic Park.
While you are staying with us, we encourage you to explore the combined 9,326 skiable acres at Park City Mountain and Deer Valley Resort. If you are looking for winter activities beyond skiing and snowboarding, may we suggest snow biking, dog sledding, fly-fishing, snowmobiling, and even stand up paddleboard yoga in a geo-thermal crater. Or you may prefer something a bit less active. If you are a history buff you will enjoy one of our museums or historic tours. You can also spend hours strolling through quaint shops and galleries on Historic Main Street.
When it's time to spice things up, enjoy mouth-watering courses at one of over 150 restaurants, sip on hand-crafted whiskey at the world’s first and only ski-in distillery, or sign up for a cooking class, food tour, or wine tasting class. When a little downtime is needed choose from a wide variety of spas and massage therapist.
However you chose to experience Park City, we encourage you to take advantage of our free bus and trolley system or use one of our transportation providers to get you from place to place.
